Choose Your Plan

Pick what fits your winter

Two straightforward options. No tricks, no fine print that’s hard to read.

Plan One
Pay As You Go
Best if you only need help a few times each winter, or want to try Snowless before committing to a full season.
  • •Price based on driveway size.
  • •Good for occasional storms or busy weeks.
  • •Driveway and front walkway included.
  • •Optional add-ons like extra paths or car snow removal.
  • •Simple payment by e-transfer after each visit.
Plan Two
Season Pass
Best if you want the whole winter handled without making calls or thinking about each storm.
  • •Full season coverage (roughly Dec–Apr).
  • •Service at about 2" / 5 cm of snow.
  • •Post-plow cleanup for the city ridge at your driveway.
  • •Text or phone updates on snow days.
  • •Priority in the route compared to pay-as-you-go calls.
